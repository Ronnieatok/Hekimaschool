Hekima International School

Educating minds, building character, and shaping futures since 2009

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Hekima International School has been a cornerstone of educational excellence in our community. We pride ourselves on nurturing young minds and preparing them for the challenges of tomorrow. Our mission is to provide a holistic education that develops critical thinking, creativity, and character in a supportive and inclusive environment.

Our Mission

To provide a transformative educational experience that empowers students to achieve their highest potential, become lifelong learners, and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leader in innovative education, developing future-ready graduates who excel academically, think critically, and act compassionately.

Our History

2009

Hekima Internation School founded with 50 students in temporary facilities

2011

Moved to current 20-acre campus with new elementary building

2018

Added high school program and STEM laboratory

2021

Awarded "Blue Ribbon School of Excellence" by the Department of Education

2024

Opened new performing arts center and robotics lab

Our Campus

Our 20-acre campus features state-of-the-art facilities designed to inspire learning and creativity:

  • Modern classrooms with smartboard technology
  • Science and robotics laboratories
  • 10,000 volume library with digital resources
  • Performing arts center with 300-seat theater
  • Athletic complex with gymnasium and sports fields
  • Outdoor learning spaces and nature trails
