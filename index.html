About Our School

Since its establishment in 2009, Hekima International School has played an important role in our community as a centre of excellence in education. For more than a decade we have proudly nurtured young Kenyan minds, preparing them with the knowledge, values, and skills to understand themselves and the world around them, and to adapt, succeed, and thrive in an ever-changing world.

At Hekima we endeavour to provide our students with a rounded education that builds critical thinking, creative potential, and strong moral character that is relevant to the realities and dreams of the Kenyan child. At Hekima we have created a supportive, learning, inclusive and transformation focused environment that enables every learner to reach their potential, navigate their identity, and make a valuable contribution to society.

At Hekima we are focused on developing future citizens who are confident and compassionate, and ultimately ready to be fully engaged leaders through their education, not only in Kenya, but for the world beyond.
